Cars / Vehicles Inventory

Here you can view all cars/vehicles that you operate. You need to have at least one car added and assigned to a car type so client can make reservations. Your clients book car type but the system assigns specific car to each reservation. If no car of the selected car type are available for the chosen period then the reservation can't be made. You can always edit the car assigned each reservation manually. To add new car/vehicle please go to the 'Add New Car' menu.
